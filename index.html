Pulihora Recipe

Contents:

  • Time:30 min
  • Serves:3 members
Making Puliora
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1/2 kg
Green Chillies 6
Ginger 1 TSP
Curry leaves 1 Bowl
Tamarind 6 TSP
Oil 1/4 Bowl
Salt As per taste
Temeric powder 1 TSP
Ground nuts 25 GMS
Chana dal 2 TSP
Urad dal 1 TSP
Mustured seeds 1TSP
Kasmiri Chillies 3 pieces

Making Pulihora

Step 1:1.Cook 1/2 kg rice in cooker.

Step 2:.Cut green chillies and ginger finely.

Step 3:Take cooked rice in a vessel, add litle curry leaves, 1tsp salt, add 1 tsp oil to turmeric powder and mix all these into cooked rice.

Step 4:.Heat 1/4th bowl oil in a pan, add ground nuts and red chillies let them fry, separate them from oil, add to rice.

Step 5:.Add 2 tsp channa dal to oil, let it fry till golden colour, now add 1 tsp Urad dal, 1&1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds, Green chillies, 1 tsp ginger pieces, 2 stems curry leaves.

Step 6:Add 6-8 tsp Tamarind paste (1 pinch sugar or Jaggery added if necesarry), 2 tsp salt and keep it for 1 minute.

Step 7:Mix the ingredients to rice thoroughly.

Step 8:This Tamarind Pulihora is ready to be served. You can have it as breakfast or as a special item on festivals.

Step 9:NOTES: The paste of the Pulihora can be stored for 1 month if it is placed in Freezer.
